Monthly Soda Brewing contest

Many people probably wonder how this town of ours got the name, Soda Springs. Well, it starts decades ago when it was still an early settlement without a name there was a shortage of safe water to drink and carbonated water was discovered and started being used widely by the surrounding states. What made us different was that many of the citizens at that time didn’t like the taste of the soda water and decided to try to make flavors to help encourage wider usage to keep everyone healthy.

Eventually, a tradition was started becoming our monthly competition to make the best tasting soda. The winner has their soda put into production in our local soda factory for as long as the community wants to have it around. The creator gets a chunk of all sales made on the soda.
