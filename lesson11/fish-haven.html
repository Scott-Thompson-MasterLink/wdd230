Fish Fights

Like any town, we have our own peculiarities one of ours is the fish in the area. While there are many behaviors we could point out the one we will talk about today is that of what appears to be mock fighting between the fish in the lakes twice a year mid-spring and mid-fall. We say mock because none of the fish injure each other and for all appearances seem to be putting on a show for those watching.

Many have speculated why the local fish do this whereas the fish of the same breeds elsewhere don’t. One popular belief is that the fish are establishing a social hierarchy with the young fish being integrated with the older fish. We have had many scientists come to study this phenomenon, but so far this seems to be another mystery yet to be solved.
