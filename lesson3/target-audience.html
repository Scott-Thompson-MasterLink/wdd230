    Target Audience

    The target audience are those who are adults and young adults that are interested in the local news, events, weather. This website will be accessible to anyone with a computer or a smartphone.

    Personas

    The active and curious adults of the communities will use this website to keep track of local events (such as an easter egg hunt or other holiday specific event), news, and the weather for thier area.

    The leaders of the communities will also be interested in submiting events that will be going on to advertise and gather more people who are interested creating more unity in the community.
